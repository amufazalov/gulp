"My artistic journey began at the age of 15, and by 17 I had already directed my first short film."

Ali Fakhrmousavi — a film director, screenwriter, and producer whose work spans the fields of feature film, documentary, theatre, and television. Born in Iran (1978), he earned a BA in Dramatic Literature from the University of Art & Architecture in Tehran and later completed an MA in Cinema at the Kyiv National I.K. Karpenko-Kary Theatre, Cinema and Television University (2005). From 2002 to 2023 he lived and worked across Europe, before relocating to Vancouver, Canada, to continue his career.

Education and Early Career

Between 1996 and 2015, Ali Fakhrmousavi gained extensive experience across film, theatre, television, and radio. He wrote and directed television series, TV films, documentaries, and journalistic programs, while also working in online production, news, and reality formats. This multidisciplinary foundation shaped the cinematic voice that later defined his feature films.

“I work with contemporary human stories across different parts of the world… Every culture inevitably shares hidden commonalities with others — it is these connections that must be uncovered and transformed into a language of mutual understanding.”

feature films

Autumn Memories (2016)

Director, Screenwriter

Ali Fakhrmousavi's first feature-length film outside Iran, Autumn Memories was shot entirely in Ukraine with sequences in English. This international co-production gave the film a unique atmosphere and underlined its universal message.

The story follows a sculptor who has lost his wife and sinks into despair, only to find new hope through a young Iraqi refugee. At once a personal drama and an anti-war love story, the film uses intimate relationships as a metaphor for the devastations and renewals experienced by entire nations.

Blue Land (2020)

Director, Screenwriter

Ali Fakhrmousavi's second feature tells the story of Dr. Arash, who leaves Tehran for a remote border village where he confronts hidden legacies of the past. The film was produced through cooperation between Iran, the Czech Republic, Italy, and Ukraine.

Selected Festivals & Awards:
  • Silver State Film Festival Best International Feature
  • L'HIFF — Barcelona International Film Festival Official Selection
  • Indo-Global International Film Festival Official Selection
