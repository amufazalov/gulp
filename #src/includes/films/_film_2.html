Blue Land (2020)

Director, Screenwriter

Ali Fakhrmousavi's second feature tells the story of Dr. Arash, who leaves Tehran for a remote border village where he confronts hidden legacies of the past. The film was produced through cooperation between Iran, the Czech Republic, Italy, and Ukraine.

Selected Festivals & Awards:
  • Silver State Film Festival Best International Feature
  • L'HIFF — Barcelona International Film Festival Official Selection
  • Indo-Global International Film Festival Official Selection
